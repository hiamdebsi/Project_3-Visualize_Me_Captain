Problem Analysis

Weather correlates to the local HQ's of NYC stock exchanges during 2012 to 2020.

A gentle breeze, a bit of rain... I don't mind this. Although, when it comes to a full out rain or snow storm, this inclement weather may devastate a life, damage property, or close off an internet connection. Did some climatic event affect your financial situation? Let us investigate if daily weather has some affect on stock markets.

Considerations

Narrowing Our Scope: We will be considering weather in the New York city area, and stock exchanges from New York, where their head quarters are in NYC as well. Let's analyse the stock close prices and the transaction volumes to see if there is a pattern for different weather conditions and a correlation that may occur over time.

Data Extraction

Weather data was collected through an api from World Weather Online, from Jan 2014 to Nov 2021 for NYC. Historic stock information (until Nov 2021) was collected through an api from Alphavantage using their daily Times Series data and an api of Fundamental Data for their company overview. GPS coordinates were collected through Google Maps and Geopy.

Data Transformation

The CSV and JSON files from our extracts required standardization to ensure data would be able to be utilized for analysis.
